How to make Breakfast

Drections and Food

  • Scrambled Eggs
    1. Turn on the stove for cooking
    2. Find out how many eggs you want
    3. Crack eggs in a bowl
    4. Mix/wisk the eggs to gether
    5. Add any spices or seasons you want
    6. Pour your mixed eggs in a pan
    7. Set you pan on the stove
    8. Watch the eggs and make sure they dont burn
    9. When the eggs are a golden color chop them up
    10. When done puts eggs on a plate and enjoy.
  • Bacon
    1. Turn on the stove for cooking
    2. Fing out how many peices of bacon you want
    3. Put your peices of bacon in the hot pan
    4. Watch the bacon so it dont burn and flip every so often
    5. When the bacon is at the crispness you want take out of pan
    6. Put bacon on a plate and enjoy
